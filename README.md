# Supplementary Animations

This repository hosts supplementary animations associated with the study:

**Ionospheric Mechanisms Leading to Plasma-Bubble Reversal and Deformation During the 12 November 2025 Geomagnetic Storm**

## Available Animations

### Animation S1 – Storm-Time ROTI Evolution
Time-resolved ROTI maps showing the development, expansion, deformation, and drift evolution of equatorial plasma bubbles (EPBs) across the American sector during the 12 November 2025 geomagnetic storm.

### Animation S2 – All-Sky Imager Observations
630.0 nm airglow observations illustrating the morphological evolution and zonal motion of plasma bubbles over South America during the storm.
